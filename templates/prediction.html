Why Predict Outcomes?

The Student Outcome Prediction System, developed at COMSATS University Attock, enables educators to forecast student performance in online courses. By entering academic, engagement, and demographic data, our advanced CNN+LSTM model predicts whether a student will Pass, Fail, Withdraw, or achieve a Distinction. This tool helps identify at-risk students early, allowing for timely interventions to boost success rates.
