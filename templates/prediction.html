Predict Student Outcome

Why Predict Outcomes?

The Design and Development of a Hybrid Deep Learning Model for Student Performance Prediction in MOOCs: Integrating Optimized Feature Selection enables educators to forecast student performance in Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs). Using a hybrid CNN+LSTM model, the system analyzes academic, engagement, and demographic data to predict outcomes (Pass, Fail, Withdrawn, Distinction). This tool helps identify at-risk students early, supporting timely interventions to enhance success rates.
