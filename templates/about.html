About the Student Outcome Prediction System

Project Overview

The Student Outcome Prediction System is a cutting-edge project developed at COMSATS University Attock to predict student performance in online courses. By analyzing academic records, online engagement, and demographic details, our system forecasts whether a student will Pass, Fail, Withdraw, or achieve a Distinction. This allows educators to identify at-risk students early and provide targeted support to improve their chances of success.

Our goal is to enhance online education by leveraging advanced machine learning, making learning more effective and accessible for students worldwide.

Objectives

  • Develop a highly accurate model to identify students who may struggle, enabling timely interventions.
  • Create an intuitive web interface for educators to input student data and receive real-time predictions.
  • Visualize key data insights through charts to help educators understand trends and make informed decisions.
  • Contribute to educational research by analyzing factors that influence student outcomes.

Methodology

Our system uses a hybrid CNN+LSTM model, combining two powerful machine learning techniques:

  1. Convolutional Neural Network (CNN): Scans student data to find patterns, like how quiz scores and forum activity relate to success.
  2. Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM): Analyzes data as a sequence, capturing trends over time, such as changes in engagement.
  3. Data Processing: We clean and standardize 12 features, ensuring the model learns effectively.
  4. Prediction: The model outputs one of four outcomes (Pass, Fail, Withdrawn, Distinction) with high accuracy.

Trained on a dataset of student records, this model outperforms traditional methods by deeply understanding complex relationships in the data.

Features and Data

We use 12 features to make predictions, grouped into three categories:

  • Academic Background: Number of previous course attempts, credits studied.
  • Engagement Metrics: Forum participation, content views, quiz attempts, resource usage.
  • Demographic Details: Education level, socio-economic indicators, age group, disability status.

These features are carefully selected based on their impact on student performance and are processed to ensure reliable predictions.

Project Goals

  • Build a robust prediction tool to support educators and students.
  • Enable early detection of at-risk students to improve retention rates.
  • Provide actionable insights through data visualizations.
  • Create a user-friendly web platform for seamless interaction with the model.

By achieving these goals, we aim to transform online education and support student success.
